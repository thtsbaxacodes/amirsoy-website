Shashleek

About

20+ shashlik variations at the heart of the resort

Shashleek is a contemporary Uzbek restaurant at the centre of Amirsoy Resort, dedicated to the art of the shashlik. The menu features over 20 variations of skewered meats, alongside classic dishes like Mastyava soup, Alatskaya samosa, and Vagury.

Traditional Uzbek patterns meet modern design in the interior — carpets, textiles, and national dishware paired with a spacious terrace offering mountain views and Eastern music.
