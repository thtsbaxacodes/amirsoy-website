Who We Are

World-class all-season mountain resort in Uzbekistan

Amirsoy Resort is located on an area of 900 hectares in one of the most beautiful and picturesque places of the capital region, on the spurs of the Chatkal Ridge in the western part of the Tian Shan Mountains, just 65 kilometers from Tashkent.

We offer a wide range of active entertainment for every taste in winter and summer. The resort's infrastructure and service will appeal to any visitor — from professional sportsmen and extreme lovers to the youngest tourists and family companies.

Infrastructure

Everything you need on the mountain

🚡

Gondola Lifts

Two modern gondola lifts — Prima reaching 2,290m summit and Amirsoy Express serving the main resort area. Both operate 9:00 – 19:00.

⛷️

Ski & Snowboard

World-class ski runs and snowboard terrain park across perfectly groomed slopes. Ski-in / ski-out access via the Express gondola lift.

🏠

Accommodations

Four chalet types: Deluxe, Premium Deluxe, Suite, and Presidential Suite. All feature open terraces, fireplaces, equipped kitchens, and breakfast.

🍽️

Restaurants

Four restaurants on site offering mountain cuisine, European fine dining, casual après-ski dining, and authentic Uzbek flavours.

🧖

Hammom & SPA

Premium Red Rock Hammom & SPA complex — recently renovated. Traditional Russian banya and wellness treatments. Open 10:00 – 00:00.

🏊

Pool & Leisure

Heated outdoor pool, concierge services, rope park (Yeti Park), and quad biking — activities for the whole family in all seasons.

Location

Chimgan, Tian Shan Mountains
