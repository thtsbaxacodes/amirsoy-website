About the chalet

A spacious two-storey chalet totalling 209 m², including a 49 m² open panoramic terrace overlooking the valley. Four en-suite bedrooms are distributed across both floors — two on each level — accommodating up to 8 guests with full privacy.

The ground floor features a living room with a 65" TV, a high-ceiling dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen. Each bedroom has its own 42" TV. All bathrooms are decorated with wood-type finishes and stone sinks, with signature Amouage amenities.

Premium mattresses with unique filler combinations ensure restorative sleep. The terrace and fireplace are the natural gathering points after a day on the slopes.
