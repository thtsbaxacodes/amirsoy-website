Privacy Policy

This policy describes how JV LLC "Amirsoy" collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Personal Information Collected

This policy applies to all data gathered by JV LLC "Amirsoy" through its website and services. By using our services, you accept these terms.

We collect two categories of information:

  • —Voluntarily provided data — information you submit during registration or when using our services. Some fields are marked as required.
  • —Automatically transmitted technical data — including your IP address, cookie information, and browser information collected during site visits.

We do not verify the accuracy of third-party information and do not control third-party websites linked from our platform. You are responsible for keeping your information current and accurate.

2. Purposes of Data Collection

We only retain information that is necessary for providing our services. Your personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • —Account identification and fulfillment of service contracts
  • —Delivery of personalized services tailored to your preferences
  • —User communication, confirmations, and service notifications
  • —Improving the quality and performance of our services
  • —Advertising of resort products and services
  • —Statistical research and analytics

3. Data Transmission & Processing

Your information is stored with protections in place against unauthorized or accidental access, destruction, modification, blocking, or disclosure.

We may share personal data with third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • —When necessary to provide the requested service
  • —In the event of a business transfer or reorganization
  • —For legal protection or compliance with applicable law

All data processing is carried out in accordance with the Law of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On Electronic Commerce."

4. Security Measures

JV LLC "Amirsoy" implements organizational and technical measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, and accidental loss. We continuously review and update our security practices to ensure your data remains protected.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be announced on our website with an updated publication date.

New versions of the policy take effect immediately upon posting. Continued use of our services following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed of any changes.
