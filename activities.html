Amirsoy Mountain Resort

Activities

Year-round adventures at 2,290 m — gondola rides, ski slopes, rope parks, quad biking, and a premium spa complex. Something for every season and every age.

🚡

Infrastructure

Gondola Lifts

Prima Gondola

An unforgettable journey to the summit of Amirsoy, reaching a height of 2,290 metres above sea level. The highest point in the resort, offering panoramic views of the Tian Shan range.

Length2,193 m
Summit altitude2,290 m
Capacity/hour2,400 persons
Operating hours9:00 – 19:00

Amirsoy Express

An exciting lift to the main resort area on a cable car accommodating 10 people. The primary connection between the base and the ski area — also the ski-in/ski-out gondola for chalet guests.

Length2,193 m
Cabin capacity10 persons
Capacity/hour2,400 persons
Operating hours9:00 – 19:00
