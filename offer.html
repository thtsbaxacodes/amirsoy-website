Amirsoy Resort

Public Offer

Public offer agreement for the purchase of goods and services from Joint Venture Limited Liability Company "Amirsoy" (INN 305152772), Tashkent region, Bostonliq district.

Terms & Definitions

Operator / Seller: Joint Venture Limited Liability Company "Amirsoy" (INN 305152772), located in Tashkent region, Bostonliq district, Chimgan Territory, Republic of Uzbekistan.

Buyer: Any individual who accepts this public offer and provides personal data for the purpose of purchasing goods or services.

Website: The official online platform operated by JV LLC "Amirsoy" through which services and goods may be purchased.

Voucher: A digital document confirming payment for services, delivered via email after successful transaction.

1. General Provisions

This document constitutes a public offer in accordance with Article 369 of the Civil Code of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Both parties confirm they possess the legal capacity to enter into this agreement.

Acceptance of this offer occurs upon completion of an order on the website. By placing an order, the buyer agrees to all terms of this offer in full.

All legal relationships arising from this offer are governed by the legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

2. Subject of the Contract

The seller undertakes to transfer ownership of goods or provide services, and the buyer undertakes to accept and pay for them in accordance with the terms of this offer.

Ownership of goods transfers to the buyer upon full payment and physical receipt of the goods or services. The risk of accidental loss transfers simultaneously.

3. Rights & Obligations of the Parties

The Seller undertakes to:

  • —Fulfill all obligations in accordance with applicable legislation
  • —Process buyer personal data confidentially and in compliance with data protection law
  • —Provide accurate information about goods and services offered

The Buyer undertakes to:

  • —Provide accurate and complete personal information during registration and ordering
  • —Pay for goods and services in full prior to use
  • —Comply with all terms of this offer and resort rules
  • —Keep account credentials confidential — credentials are non-transferable

4. Registration

To access purchasing services, users must register with their full name, a valid email address, and a self-created password.

Completing the registration process constitutes full acceptance of all conditions set out in this public offer.

5. Purchase of Goods & Services

All goods and services purchased through the website are valid exclusively at the Amirsoy Mountain Resort facilities.

The terms and conditions of a purchase cannot be modified after the transaction has been completed.

6. Using Purchased Items

Upon successful payment, a voucher will be delivered to the buyer's registered email address.

Vouchers contain a digital QR code that enables access to purchased services at resort facilities. Present the QR code at the relevant service point upon arrival.

7. Payment

All prices on the website are listed in Uzbek sum (UZS).

Payment is processed via UZCARD card transfer through the website's secure payment interface.

Unilateral changes to prices by the seller do not affect purchases that have already been confirmed and paid for.

8. Returns & Exchanges

Refund requests must be submitted via a written application at the resort cashier.

The seller will review refund requests on a case-by-case basis, taking into consideration circumstances of non-use and any applicable weather-related closures.

Refunds are not guaranteed and are issued solely at the discretion of resort management in accordance with applicable Uzbek law.

9. Force Majeure

Neither party shall be held liable for failure to fulfill obligations resulting from circumstances beyond their reasonable control, including natural disasters, acts of government, or other force majeure events. The affected party must notify the other as soon as possible.

10. Intellectual Property

All content on the Amirsoy website — including text, images, logos, graphics, and software — is the exclusive intellectual property of JV LLC "Amirsoy" and is protected by the copyright laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan. Unauthorized reproduction or use is prohibited.

11. Dispute Resolution

All disputes arising from this offer shall first be addressed through negotiation between the parties.

If no resolution is reached through negotiation, disputes shall be resolved through the courts of the Republic of Uzbekistan in accordance with applicable law.
